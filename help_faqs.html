Help & FAQS

Order
Will i recieve in a order confirmation ?

Upon successful placement of your order, you will receive an automatic acknowledgement of receipt via e-mail. In case you did not receive the order confirmation, please contact us via the contact form on our website.

are the products i select always deliverable ?

Usually, only items that are actually available are shown in our AlphaTauri online shop. Unavailable sizes are shown in grey. Although our database is updated on an ongoing basis, in exceptional cases, a certain item might be sold out between the product selection and the checkout confirmation. In very rare cases, a product may not be available when we pack your order. If this happens, we will notify you immediately.

can i order by phone or email ?

Please understand that currently we cannot accept orders placed by phone or e-mail. You can order 24/7 in the AlphaTauri online shop.

Payment
what payment option do you have?

Your payment options might differ depending on your country and delivery address. You will find your available options in the payment section of the checkout. Credit cards: Visa, MasterCard PayPal: In order to be able to use this service you must register with PayPal (free of charge). For more information on PayPal, please click on the following link: www.paypal.com Direct eBanking: - For orders placed in Germany and Austria - In order to be able to use this service, all you need is an online banking account at your bank and your login data. - With direct eBanking you will instruct your bank to execute a transfer. - No registration necessary - For more information on direct eBanking, please click here We protect your sensitive information using SSL encryption (Secure Socket Layer) and the 3D Secure service. All credit card data is transmitted to us in encrypted form. Payment on invoice: - For orders placed in Germany and Austria - Pay within 14 days of receipt of your order. For this payment method, you will need to supply your date of birth. - Payments on invoice are processed by Arvato Supply Chain Solutions SE. IDEAL: - For orders placed in the Netherlands. - In order to be able to use iDEAL you need to be a customer of the participating banks. - Payments can be done using the mobile banking app or the online banking environment of your own bank

where can i find my invoice ?

As soon as your order is dispatched, you will receive an e-mail confirmation including the invoice and a tracking code. Should you not receive your invoice, please contact us here.
